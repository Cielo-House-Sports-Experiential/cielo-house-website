About Us

Australia's Premium Experiential and Events Agency. Founder Led and Delivered

Cielo House is a premium experiential and events agency headquartered on the Gold Coast, delivering across Australia. Founded by Britt Niven, the agency works across two pillars, brand activations and premium hospitality, with a track record proven at the country's biggest sporting moments. Every event is founder led and founder delivered.

Our Exceptional Standard

Proven to deliver an exceptional standard in events

Cielo House earned its track record in the environments where the production standard has to be highest and the margin for error is lowest. More than forty years of combined experience at major events teaches a team where the pressure points actually live, and that is where the Cielo House standard was built.

Brand Activations

From concept to design, management and logistics through to delivery and post event reporting, Cielo House designs and delivers brand experiences at major sporting events for your customers and fans.

Meet the Founder

Forty Years Combined Experience
in the Industry

Britt Niven has worked in sports activation, events, and hospitality for over twenty years, and brings together a team that complements this experience. She has managed more than $30 million in event budgets, partnered with over 120 brands, and worked across the Boost Mobile Gold Coast 500, the Formula 1 Australian Grand Prix, the Supercars Championship and beyond. When it comes to events in the sports industry, Britt is an expert in delivering the best brand experiences in the country.
