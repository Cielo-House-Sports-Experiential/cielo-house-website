FAQ

Common Questions

What makes a Cielo House premium experience different? +

Every experience is built from scratch we don't sell packages. We start with your guests: who they are, what they love, what would genuinely surprise them. Then we design and execute every detail ourselves, with a dedicated concierge ensuring nothing falls through the cracks. The result is something your guests genuinely can't get anywhere else.

Can you create experiences at major sporting events internationally? +

Yes we have experience curating multi-day VIP trips to international and domestic bucket-list events. We manage the full itinerary: flights, accommodation, access, experiences, dining, and concierge service throughout. Your guests simply show up and enjoy.

How do you source talent and sporting legend access? +

Through years working in the Australian sports and event industry, we've built genuine relationships with sporting organisations, talent management agencies, and athletes directly. This network allows us to facilitate access and experiences that aren't available through standard channels.

What is the lead time required to plan a premium experience? +

For multi-day VIP trips and experiences at major events, we recommend a minimum of 8 12 weeks lead time to secure the best access, accommodation, and talent. For single-event experiences, 4 6 weeks is often sufficient. We always encourage reaching out as early as possible to maximise what we can create.
