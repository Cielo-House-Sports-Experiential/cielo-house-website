FAQ

Common Questions

How do you select and vet promotional models? +

Every person in our talent pool has been personally interviewed and assessed not just on appearance, but on professionalism, communication skills, reliability, and their ability to represent a brand authentically. We maintain a curated talent database across Australia and we personally review every casting decision against your brief.

What is your briefing process for campaign-specific knowledge? +

We develop a detailed brand briefing document for each campaign covering product knowledge, brand voice, engagement scripts, FAQs, prohibited topics, and specific event instructions. All talent completes briefing review before deployment and attends an on-site induction. For ongoing programs, we provide additional training sessions.

Can you provide staff for events in regional areas? +

Yes we have sourced and managed talent for events in regional Queensland, NSW, and Victoria. For regional deployments we factor in travel, accommodation, and per diems, and we maintain the same briefing and quality standards regardless of location.

What is the difference between a promotional model and a brand ambassador? +

Promotional models are typically engaged for specific events they represent your brand on the day with presence, engagement, and professionalism. Brand ambassadors are longer-term relationships: they develop deep knowledge of your brand, appear at multiple events over a season or campaign period, and often contribute to social content as genuine advocates. Both are managed through Cielo House with the same level of care.
