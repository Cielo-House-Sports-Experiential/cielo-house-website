What We Do

What Experiential Activations Includes

From concept through to bump-out, Cielo House designs and delivers brand experiences at major sporting properties. Race day activations, fan engagement zones, sponsor inventory build-out, and onsite content capture.

Every activation is planned around the specific venue, the specific crowd, and the specific outcome your brand needs on the day.

Our Capabilities

What's Included

Sponsorship Strategy

Activation brief development, ROI frameworks, and measurement plans across a season. Before anything is built, we establish what success looks like at the venue level and the business level.

Activation Concept and Design

Creative concept, brand zone design, look and feel, signage and wayfinding, and fan engagement mechanics built specifically for the sporting environment. Not a generic creative execution adapted to a venue. A concept designed from the venue outward.

Production and Build

Fabrication, fit-out, AV, lighting, logistics, and supplier coordination. Every asset built to withstand a race weekend environment: the heat, the crowd, the timeline, and the bump-out window that follows.

Onsite Delivery

Bump in, live run, bump out, run sheets, crew management, and post-event reconciliation. A race weekend does not give you a second chance at first impressions. The team on the floor has been briefed, drilled, and is standing at the right gate at the right time.

Activation Content

On-event content capture, social calendar, behind-the-scenes coverage, and post-event edits. The activation itself is the lead. The content that follows it extends the brand moment from race day into the weeks that come after.

Gold Coast Access

Cielo House carries established relationships with Gold Coast sporting properties, venues, and event organisers. That is a specific competitive advantage: access to the Gold Coast 500 paddock and relationships built across Supercars rounds.
