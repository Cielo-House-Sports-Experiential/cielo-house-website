Experiential Activations

Your Brand,
Inside the Moment

From concept to design, management and logistics through to delivery and post event reporting, Cielo House designs and delivers brand experiences at major sporting events for your customers and fans.

What We Do

What Experiential Activations Includes

Cielo House has a proven track record in delivering highly engaging and interactive brand activations from event day activations, fan engagement zones, consumer promotions, content capture and more.

Every activation is planned around the specific venue, the specific crowd, and the specific outcome your brand needs on the day.

Our Capabilities

What's Included

Sponsorship Strategy

Activation brief development, ROI frameworks, and measurement plans across a season. Before anything is built, we establish what success looks like for you and your brand.

Activation Concept and Design

Creative concept, brand design, look and feel, signage and branding, and fan engagement mechanics built specifically for the sporting environment. We do not create a generic creative execution, we build a custom concept designed to align with your brand and objectives.

Production and Build

From fabrication to fit out, AV and lighting, and logistics to supplier coordination, every asset is built to align with your goals, the venue and the experience desired.

Onsite Delivery

Bump in, the main event, bump out, run sheets, crew management, and post event reconciliation — we cover it all. First impressions mean everything at a brand activation, and we ensure it is nailed the first time, every time.

Activation Content

Event content capture, social planning, behind-the-scenes coverage, and post event edits — we ensure that while the activation is the key piece, the content extends the brand moment from event day into the weeks that follow.

Exclusive Expertise and Access

Cielo House carries established relationships with sporting organisations, venues and suppliers across Australia, giving us a competitive advantage in providing expertise and access across your events.
