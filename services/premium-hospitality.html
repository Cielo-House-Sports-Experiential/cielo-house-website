Premium Hospitality

The Room Your Guests Remember

Premium hospitality is not a finish. It is a sequence designed in reverse: from the moment your guest leaves to the moment they arrive, every step planned so that nothing defaults to the venue standard. Cielo House designs and delivers branded sports hospitality at Australia's major sporting events.

What We Do

What Premium Hospitality Includes

Branded lounges, hosted programmes, talent integration, and guest experiences built for the sport environment. A race weekend does not begin at the green flag.

It begins at the kerb on Thursday morning, and the hospitality standard has to hold all the way through. Cielo House plans the full sequence, from arrival to departure, so nothing is left to the venue default.

Our Capabilities

What's Included

Programme Design

Hospitality strategy, guest tier design, programme calendar, and invitation and RSVP architecture. The guest experience is planned before any furniture is ordered or any crew is briefed.

Branded Lounges and Suites

Lounge concept, fit-out, branding, furniture, food and beverage direction, and supplier curation. A branded lounge at a race weekend is a commercial asset. It needs to hold its production standard for three days, across variable crowd pressure.

Guest Experience

Arrival sequence, in-room journey, departure flow, gifting, and dwell-time choreography. Most lounges fail at 4pm, not at the gate. The 4pm problem is a programme design problem, not a catering problem.

Talent and Hosts

Talent integration, host casting, brief preparation, ambassador and promotional crew, and onsite management. The right talent in the right room at the right time is a precision exercise.

Onsite Service

Crew management, dress standards, briefing, service standards, and live floor management. The crew on the floor is the guest experience. Cielo House trains, briefs, and manages the team from the first shift to the final bump-out.

Sport Environment

Sport sets the standard differently. A race weekend lounge has to operate in heat, noise, and timeline pressure that a conference venue never encounters. Cielo House builds hospitality for sport because that is where the complexity is highest.
