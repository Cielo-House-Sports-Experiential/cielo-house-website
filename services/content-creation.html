FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do we receive content after the event? +

For social media content we offer same-day and next-day delivery options optimised images and short-form video ready to post while the conversation is still live. Full highlight reels and edited video packages are typically delivered within 5 10 business days depending on scope.

Can content creation be added to another Cielo House service? +

Absolutely content creation integrates seamlessly with brand activations, corporate hospitality, and premium experiences. Many clients choose to bundle services so their event is documented as part of the same production team. This ensures the content reflects the full brand narrative, not just isolated moments.

Do you create content for both brand and internal audiences? +

Yes we create content for both external brand channels (Instagram, TikTok, media) and internal use (team updates, board presentations, stakeholder reports). We tailor the visual style, edit, and framing based on the intended audience.

Are your content creators briefed on brand guidelines? +

Always. Before every shoot, our content team receives a full briefing document covering your brand guidelines, key messaging, talent and product focus, approved visual style, and specific deliverable requirements. Every piece of content we produce is aligned to your brand standards no exceptions.
