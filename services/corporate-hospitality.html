FAQ

Common Questions

What does a corporate hospitality experience typically include? +

Every program is bespoke, but typically includes a hospitality space design and branding, guest list management, premium catering curation, professional hosting, VIP coordination, styling, content capture, and post event reporting. We build the program around your client relationships and brand positioning.

Can you deliver hospitality programs across multiple events in a season? +

Absolutely. We specialise in national hospitality series managing consistent brand experience standards across multiple events throughout a season or calendar year. We build scalable programs and designs that maintain quality and brand integrity whether it's one event or twelve.

What types of events are suited to corporate hospitality programs? +

Sporting events are the most common, however we also deliver for music festivals, gala dinners, conferences, and bespoke brand events. The key is the quality of the client experience, not the specific event format.

How do you demonstrate the ROI of a hospitality investment? +

We work with you upfront to define success metrics aligned to your business goals such as relationship strengthening, deal pipeline, guest satisfaction scores, and brand perception. Post event we deliver a comprehensive insights report covering all agreed KPIs with strategic recommendations for future programs.
