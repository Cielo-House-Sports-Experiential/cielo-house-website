Frequently Asked Questions

What does a corporate hospitality experience include? +

Every program is bespoke, and a typical program includes hospitality space design and branding, guest list management, premium catering, professional hosting, VIP coordination, styling, content capture, and post event reporting. Cielo House builds each program around your client relationships and your brand positioning.

Can you deliver hospitality programs across multiple events in a season? +

Cielo House specialises in season-long hospitality programs, holding one consistent brand experience standard across multiple events through a season or calendar year. Programs and designs are built to scale, protecting quality and brand integrity whether the calendar holds one event or twelve.

What types of events suit corporate hospitality programs? +

Corporate hospitality works at any event where the guest experience matters, from major sporting moments to music festivals, gala dinners, conferences, and bespoke brand events. The quality of the client experience drives the outcome, not the event format.

How does Cielo House demonstrate the ROI of a hospitality investment? +

Success measures are defined upfront and aligned to your business goals, whether that is relationship strengthening, deal pipeline, guest satisfaction, or brand perception. Every program closes with a comprehensive insights report covering the agreed measures, with strategic recommendations for future programs.
