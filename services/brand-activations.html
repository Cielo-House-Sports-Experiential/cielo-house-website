FAQ

Common Questions

What types of brand activations does Cielo House deliver? +

We deliver the full spectrum from large-scale sponsorship activations at major sporting events like Supercars and the Australian Grand Prix, to targeted fan engagement experiences, product sampling campaigns, branded pop-up structures, and digital-first activations designed for social reach. Every activation is tailored to your brand goals.

Do you manage the creative concept as well as the on-site execution? +

Yes we're an end-to-end agency. We handle strategy development, creative concepting, production design, logistics, staffing, on-site management, and post-event reporting. You brief us on your objectives and we handle everything else.

Which cities and events do you activate at? +

We operate nationally across Gold Coast, Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and beyond. We've activated at major sporting events including the Supercars Championship, Formula 1 Australian Grand Prix, World Surf League events, and AFL and we're experienced at scaling campaigns across multiple venues simultaneously.

How do you measure the success of a brand activation? +

We align KPIs to your business objectives upfront whether that's brand impressions, social engagement, lead capture, product trials, or media coverage. After every activation we deliver a full amplification and reporting package so you can clearly see the impact and ROI.
