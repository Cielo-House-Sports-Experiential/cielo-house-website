Frequently Asked Questions

What types of brand activations does Cielo House deliver? +

Cielo House delivers the full spectrum of brand activations, from large scale activations at major events to fan engagement experiences, product sampling campaigns, immersive events, takeover moments, branded pop up structures, and social first activations built for digital reach. Every activation is tailored to your brand objectives and the audience in front of it.

Do you manage the creative concept as well as the onsite execution? +

Cielo House delivers the full chain end to end, from strategy and creative concept through production design, logistics, and staffing to onsite management and post event reporting. You bring the objectives, and The Cielo Team carries everything from there.

Where do you activate? +

Cielo House operates nationally, delivering brand activations across the Gold Coast, Brisbane, Sydney, Melbourne, and beyond, with proven experience scaling campaigns across multiple venues at once. The agency is headquartered on the Gold Coast and also delivers across the APAC region, holding the same standard in every market.

How do you measure the success of a brand activation? +

Success metrics are aligned to your business objectives before anything is built, whether that is brand impressions, social engagement, lead capture, product trials, or media coverage. Every activation closes with a full amplification and reporting package, so the impact and ROI of every dollar are clear.
