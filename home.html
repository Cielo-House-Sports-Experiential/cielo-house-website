Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Britt Niven

Founder & Managing Director, Cielo House

I built Cielo House to do one thing exceptionally well - deliver brand experiences that hold a premium standard for all our clients. Across twenty years in activations, hospitality, and sponsorship at the biggest events in Australia and the United States, I have managed more than $30 million in event budgets and partnered with over 120 brands, with work across the Supercars Championship, Formula 1 Australian Grand Prix, NBA All Star, Made in America Festival, Nascar Chase and the NHL and MLB.

Every program that carries the Cielo House name is founder led, from concept to post event. Cielo translates as sky in Italian, and the work we build lives up to that name.
