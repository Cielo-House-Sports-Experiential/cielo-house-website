Brand-True Signature · Designer Notes

Palette: Single navy field at #2B317B with a tonal gradient toward #20265E (deeper shade of primary, still on-brand) plus a soft #668DC6 radial wash in the corners — gives depth without introducing any off-brand tones. White for type. No muddy mid-blues.

Composition: Identity panel tightened (less wasted negative space). Logo column widened (380px) and now contains three stacked pieces: the stacked white wordmark, a sky-blue Visit Website CTA pill, and large white social pills (Instagram + LinkedIn).

Type: Bebas Neue for the name. Poppins for everything else — role in sky blue uppercase tracked, contact values at 26 px (big and legible), labels at 13 px in sky blue, slogan in italic Poppins Light.

Three clickable destinations: the Visit Website pill + the two social pills + the email and phone links all carry proper hrefs. All three social links open in new tabs.

Production export: capture as PNG at full 1980 × 480 for image-based signatures, or convert to nested-table HTML for clickable in-mail use.
